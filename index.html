Harnessing AI to transform neurodivergence identification and support.

Our mission is to use powerful, AI-enabled technology to improve early neurodivergence identification. Our core product is an innovative, game-based diagnostic tool designed to provide a new pathway to support, helping to prevent future mental health conditions and improve long-term outcomes for children and their families.

Our Technology

We've developed a novel digital screening tool for neurodivergence, specifically autism, through an interactive iPad game. This game captures precise user movement data, which is then securely transmitted to our cloud-based AI system for sophisticated analysis. This allows us to identify subtle motor patterns indicative of neurodivergence with a high degree of accuracy and reliability.

Validated and Reliable

Our solution is built upon years of rigorous, evidence-based research and development by researchers at Strathclyde University. The AI algorithms have already demonstrated reliable diagnostic performance in a multi-site Phase 3 clinical trial. This high-quality, ethically sourced data forms the bedrock of our diagnostic accuracy and sets us apart from competitors.

Wider Impact

Our project aims to deliver significant benefits beyond our company. By facilitating early identification, we enable timely access to support for children and families. This tool also empowers educators to become a more effective first line of identification, reducing pressure on NHS diagnostic services and promoting a more inclusive, preventative approach to public mental health.

Key Deliverables

This project is designed to deliver a set of tangible, innovative outputs that will directly address the critical market need for early neurodivergence identification.

Market-Ready iPad Game

A child-friendly and engaging iPad game application, refined based on user feedback from UK school pilots, with on-device data pre-processing capabilities.

Robust Cloud-Based AI

A secure, GDPR-compliant cloud infrastructure with advanced AI models for the analysis of movement data to accurately identify neurodivergent traits.

Go-to-Market Strategy

A comprehensive go-to-market strategy and commercial model, informed by our feasibility study and market consultancy services.

Support Pathway

The establishment of a practical pathway for early support and referral to NHS services for children identified as likely neurodivergent.
